The BeforeA Fragmented & Obsolete Digital Landscape

The client, a prominent player in the promotional products industry, was hampered by a combination of legacy systems and a lack of modern functionalities across their entire digital presence.

🌐

Disjointed Infrastructure

Three separate, dated websites with inconsistent branding, poor UX, and massive management overhead.

⚠

Operational Gaps

No automated tracking. New product integrations required custom code at ~$2,000 per product.

📱

Not Mobile-Ready

None of the sites were mobile-responsive. Product customization required 10 screens to complete an order.

📊

Zero Analytics

No connection to Google or Meta dashboards, making it impossible to measure ad performance or make data-driven decisions.

🔌

Outdated CMS

An extremely dated CMS prevented basic customer service functions and efficient content management.

💥

Failure Risk

Old, fragile IT infrastructure posed a substantial risk of system failure and required a costly overhaul.

The Solution:Building a Unified, Innovative Digital Core

Our solution involved a comprehensive overhaul focusing on consolidation, modernization, and innovation - merging three legacy brands into a single, powerful platform.

🌟 New Unified Brand

Strategic rebrand uniting all three legacy businesses under one cohesive identity on a scalable eCommerce platform.

⚡ 1-Page Customization

Revolutionary product customization engine reduced the process from 10 screens down to a single page.

📈 Advanced Analytics

Integrated Google & Meta dashboards providing real-time insights into traffic, behavior, and ad performance.

🎓 SEO & UX Tools

Built with best-in-class SEO and UX tools to improve organic traffic and boost conversion rates.

🔥 Marketing Toolkit

Powerful promotions and marketing tools empowering the team to run targeted campaigns efficiently.

🌟 Modern CMS

Modern CMS empowered customer service teams with essential management functions and improved satisfaction.

The Results:Cost Savings, Accelerated Growth & Digital Empowerment

The transformation delivered immediate and long-term benefits, dramatically improving operational efficiency and market position.

$100K

Immediate Cost Avoidance

Eliminated the need for costly server replacements by retiring the old infrastructure entirely.

Per-Product Dev Cost

Eliminated the $2,000 per-product custom coding cost for all new product development.
