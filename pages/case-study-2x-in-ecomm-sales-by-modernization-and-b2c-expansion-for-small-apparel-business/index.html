Case Study - eCommerce - Apparel

2x in eComm Sales by Modernization and B2C Expansion for Small Apparel Business

A complete platform migration from a legacy custom build to a modern, scalable BigCommerce solution - opening new channels, transforming brand presence, and delivering 2x sales growth in just 2 years.

The Situation: Crippled by Legacy Technology

The client - a respected firm in the apparel industry - was severely limited by a custom e-commerce platform that resembled the early days of the internet. Growth had stalled, and the technology had become a liability.

Poor User Experience

A dated menu, low-resolution and inconsistent images, confusing navigation, and a complete lack of search functionality.

No eCommerce Foundation

Product pages had non-existent SEO, zero descriptions, and poor quality photography - undermining product value entirely.

Security and Maintenance Risk

Reliance on dated, third-party IT infrastructure left the website vulnerable to cyber-attacks with no clear owner.

Stagnant Business Growth

Sales were driven by an aging customer base. The static site could not attract new customers or adapt to modern market needs.


The Transformation: A Scalable, Feature-Rich Platform

The core solution involved a complete migration to BigCommerce - chosen over Shopify for its robust support for 600+ product variations, advanced multi-tier price lists, and seamless integration with the client's existing payment provider.

Strategic Platform Selection

BigCommerce selected for advanced B2B catalog management, tiered pricing by user login, and existing payment provider compatibility.

Technical Implementation

Built a strong SEO foundation and modern, intuitive UX. Integrated cutting-edge B2B and B2C tools to streamline operations.

Marketing and CRM

Seamless HubSpot integration for unified CRM and marketing automation, connecting all sales and customer data in one place.

Merchandising Upgrades

3D product models, vendor mapping, and a robust promotional engine to enhance discovery and drive conversion.


The Results: Holistic Growth and Sales Doubling

The modernized platform delivered significant, measurable benefits across the entire organization - from sales performance to brand perception.

Key Outcome

Achieved 2x growth in eCommerce sales in just 2 years - a transformational result driven by platform modernization, new channel expansion, and a dramatically improved customer experience.

Enhanced Customer Experience

A modern, fast, and intuitive site with powerful product pages, quick navigation, and up-to-date B2B and B2C tools.

Stronger Brand Identity

Full company story - vision, leadership, and value proposition - elevated with better visuals and a cohesive brand design.

Holistic Stakeholder Benefit

The new site was praised by partners, improved recruiting efforts, and made the company feel better represented online.
