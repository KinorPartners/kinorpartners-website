5-STEP PROCESS

How To Wear The Shirt Tucker

A discreet under-belt that grips your shirt and anchors it below your waistband - no more re-tucking.

💡 First-time tip
The button feels stiff on first use - stretch the hole wider, push firmly, and it clicks. By the second use it takes under 5 seconds.
1
Wrap Around Your Waist
Smooth side facing the body. Find the hole that matches your waist.
2
Loosen the Rubber
Stretch where the button meets the holes - only needed on first use.
3
Insert the Button
Push the button through the hole - flex pegs lock it in place.
4
Tuck the Slack
Fold the remaining slack flat against your back behind the belt.
5
Put Pants On Top
Pants and your regular belt go over it - completely hidden, all-day hold.
FIRST-TIME SETUP

How To Insert The Button

The first time can feel stiff - here's the trick. Takes 10 seconds once you know it.

1
Wrap to Find Your Length
Wrap around your waist, smooth side in. Identify the hole that matches your waist size.
2
Stretch the Hole First
Stretch the area where the button meets the hole - only needed on the first use.
3
Push the Button In
Push firmly into the stretched hole - flex pegs click and lock it in place.
💡 Pro tip
Stretch wider than you need, push firmly, click. After the first use the rubber breaks in and it's effortless.
