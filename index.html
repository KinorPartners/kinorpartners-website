The Growth Gap

Why most small businesses fail to capture the potential with AI across marketing, sales, and Ecommerce.

Urgent Fires vs. Strategic Growth

Small teams are often buried in "burning" operational issues—returns, defects, and daily fires. This urgency crowds out the important work of building a new Amazon sales channel, leaving future growth on permanent standby.

The Disqualifying Start-Up Cost

Building internal expertise is slow and risky; hiring top-tier specialists is prohibitively expensive. For many, the upfront investment required to "do it right" kills the business case before the first listing even goes live.

Perceived Risk & Logistics Maze

Experimenting with AI takes time and missteps may lead to unintended downsides. Amazon's ecosystem is a logistical labyrinth. Without expert hand-holding, the fear of account suspensions, lost inventory, and wasted ad spend makes the channel feel too risky to touch.
